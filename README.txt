# Welcome to the Backrooms
## Platforms:
PC
## Target Age:
16+
## Rating: 
teen or higher
## Game Summary:
Our protagonist “Mike” is 17 years old who hasn’t seen his friends in a while. The last known location was an abandoned office building. Tired of not knowing where they were he traveled to the building and phased into the backrooms. He wanders around in the first level finding items that belonged to their friends. He decides to explore the area in search of his friends. Throughout his journey he encounters monsters of different levels and remnants of his friends. Until he finally finds his last friend. He's alive but something's wrong with him
